About Me

An educator passionate about technology and science

Hi, I'm Khusni Ja'far, an educator with a love for learning and reading that has inspired a growing passion for teaching, especially in the fields of math, physics, and informatics. I enjoy sparking students' curiosity about how the world works through physics, guiding their mathematical thinking and problem-solving, while also nurturing creative coding and computational thinking through platforms like Scratch and web development. I'm excited to contribute to shaping future scientifically minded learners and digital talents through meaningful and engaging learning experiences.

My Educational Philosophy

My approach to teaching and student development

I believe that every student is intelligent and possesses unique talents. I do not favor only one or two of the most academically gifted students in my class, nor do I look down on those who struggle to keep up. In my view, students who excel in and enjoy my subject likely have a natural interest or inclination in that area.

However, I remain committed to professionally delivering lessons in a way that helps all students understand the material effectively.

Each student has their own path to success—some may thrive working in offices, others as scientists, content creators, athletes, artists, and so on. Therefore, I do not force all students to become experts in the subject I teach. I recognize that every student has different types of intelligence and unique talents.
